BUbble shooter

With a history of popularity and demand, bubble games are a popular category of light video games due to its ease of use. Their play sessions are brief, appealing to all ages, and easily accessible. Bubble games are still a popular option for short bursts of fun entertainment because of their stress-relieving qualities. (Kurkina, 2021)

  • Aim: use arrows to the direction you wanna aim the bubble
  • shoot: use space to shoot in the direction you aimed
  • Use walls to bounce your bubbles for tricky shots.
  • Avoid hitting bombs to prevent game over.
  • Stones act as obstacles. You cannot match or remove them.
